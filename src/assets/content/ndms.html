Challenge

NDMS Content Management System

NASCAR Digital Media Services, a creative unit within NASCAR digital, needed a content management system to sell to a wide range of clients. Many had limited resources and modest budgets. Deep collaboration between design and development produced a flexible, easy to use product that could be reproduced quickly, but still provide customization for the client.

Client

NASCAR Digital Media

Roles

Visual Design / UI, UX / Art Direction

Solutions

Wordpress infrastructure provides robust support, stability and easy to use interface
Flexible publishing and multimedia tools well suited for diverse list of clients
Reusable components cut cost but can be restyled for custom look
Grid and responsive designs support mobile experiences

Execution
